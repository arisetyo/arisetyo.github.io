Mgmt!

20+ years in managing products, projects, and people.

Straight out of uni in the early 2000's I immediately started doing freelance projects (check out the "Red Plate" section). A few years down the road, me and my long-time collaborator created our own brand of digital agency, “Galenic”. We did everything in tandem; from selling our services, design solutions, to hands-on coding. My partner in Galenic now runs his own amazing digital agency.

In 2013, I took the role of Head of Engineering in “Ohdio”, a local music streaming service, based in Jakarta, backed by East Ventures. I was responsible for leading the engineering team, managing product development, and ensuring the technical success of the platform.

Some of my experiences in management includes:

  • as Head of Engineering in Zenius (2023), one of my main duties were taking immediate actions to reduce their infrastructure cost

Music and media

Code + passion

I can empashize enough how music has been a big part of my life. I've been listening to my parents' cassette tapes since I was 6, playing musical instruments since I was 8, performed in bands since I was 14, and written and produced songs for demo albums since I was 20. Music is a life-long passion of mine.

So it is with great pleasure that I had the chance to work with some projects related to music and media:

Ohdio

Ohdio, a music streaming service based in Jakarta, was my first foray into the music sector of in the tech industry. I joined them as Head of Engineering, with a minority stake in the company, back in 2013.

Gamelan

Gamelan is a desktop music player we at Galenic developed since 2010. It's a simple music player that plays music from your local storage. What differentiates it from other music players is that it automatically gathers information regarding the song currently playing from public API like Last.fm, EchoNest, Flickr, and others. This give listeners contextual and richer information about the song, the artist, and the album.

Irama Nusantara

Irama Nusantara is music streaming service that focuses on Indonesian music up to the 1970's. It was a project in 2015 I did with a friend of mine, one of the co-founders of Ohdio, who handled the UIX design. Irama Nusantara is an initiative to preserve and promote Indonesian music heritage.
